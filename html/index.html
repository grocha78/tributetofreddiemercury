Freddie Mercury

  • September 5, 1946 - Freddie Mercury is born as Frederick Bulsara in the East African island of Zanzibar
  • 1953 - Young Frederick starts taking piano lessons
  • 1964 - Family moves to Middlesex
  • Late '60s - While studying at Ealing College of Art in Middlesex, Freddie is introduced to Roger Taylor and Brian May and joins their band, Smile, as lead vocalist
  • 1971 - John Deacon joins the band as bassist and the iconic Queen, as we know it, is formed
  • 1973 - Their debut album, "Queen", is released and is hailed as one of the most innovative developments ever in rock music
